Introduction

At Creed Cart, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase from our store.

What Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you create an account, place an order, or sign up for our newsletter, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping addresses, and payment information.

Non-Personal Information:We may collect non-identifiable data such as IP address, device type, browser type, and information about your interaction with the website (via cookies or tracking technologies).

How We Use Your Information

Order Fulfilment: To process and ship your orders and send you order updates.

Communication: To provide customer service, respond to your inquiries, and send order-related notifications.

Marketing: With your consent, we may send you promotional emails, discounts, and updates about our products and services.

Improvement: To enhance our website, products, and customer service.

Data Protection

We implement security measures, including encryption and secure payment systems, to protect your personal data during transactions

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your data with third-party service providers for purposes like payment processing, shipping, and marketing. These partners are required to keep your information secure.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting our support team.

You may opt-out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, track usage, and personalise content. You can manage cookies in your browser settings




Note: Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Effective Date."
