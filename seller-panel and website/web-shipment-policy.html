    Refund Requests

    To initiate a refund request, please contact our customer support team at support@smart-solution.com within the specified eligibility period. Provide your order number, a detailed reason for the refund request, and any supporting documentation, if necessary.

    Non-Refundable Items

    Certain products or services may not be eligible for a refund. These include, but are not limited to, digital downloads, customized products, and services that have already been rendered.

    Processing Time

    Once your refund request is received, we will review it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. The processing time may vary, but we strive to complete the review within 15 days business days.

    Refund Method

    Approved refunds will be processed using the original method of payment. Please allow 15 business days for the refund to be reflected in your account.

    Cancelled Orders

    If you cancel an order before it is processed, a full refund will be issued. However, if the order has already been processed, it will be treated as a standard refund request.

    Contact Information

    For any questions regarding our Refunds Policy, please contact us at support@smart-solution.com.

    Policy Updates

    Smart Solution reserves the right to update or modify this Refunds Policy at any time. Please check our website for the latest version.

    By making a purchase with Smart Solution, you acknowledge and agree to comply with our Refunds Policy. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
