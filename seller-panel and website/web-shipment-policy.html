Shipping Locations

Domestic Shipping:Creed Cart offers nationwide shipping within the territory of Pakistan.

Excluded Regions:Some countries or regions may be excluded from shipping due to restrictions or logistical challenges. (FATA, FANA)

Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

Standard Shipping:The typical delivery time for standard shipping is usually 5-7 business days for domestic orders, depending on your location.

Expedited Shipping: However we offer faster shipping options, such as 2-day or next-day delivery, for an additional fee.

Shipping Partners:Creed Cart works with trusted shipping carriers or local postal services for deliveries.

Shipping Costs

Tiered shipping cost:Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, size, and destination of your order, and may vary accordingly.

Order Processing Time

Order Processing: Once an order is placed, it typically takes 1-2 business days for the order to be processed and shipped. Processing times may vary during peak shopping seasons (e.g., holidays or sales events).

Delays:Unforeseen delays due to high demand, weather conditions, or issues with the shipping carrier may extend delivery times.

Tracking Orders

If you cancel an order before it is processed, a full refund will be issued. However, if the order has already been processed, it will be treated as a standard refund request.

Contact Information

Tracking Information:After an order is shipped, customers are usually provided with a tracking number via email. This allows customers to monitor the status of their package.

Track Your Order:You can track the status of your shipment directly on the carrier’s website using the provided tracking number.

Shipping Restrictions

P.O. Boxes:Some carriers may not deliver to P.O. Box addresses, so it’s important to ensure your shipping address is accurate and eligible for delivery.

Order Issues or Shipping Errors

Missing or Damaged Items:If your order is lost, damaged, or incomplete upon arrival, you should immediately contact customer service.

Contact Information:If there is a shipping issue, contacting Creed Cart's customer service team will help resolve any problems quickly.

Signature Requirement

Signature on Delivery: For all items, Creed Cart requires a signature upon delivery to ensure that the package is safely received by the customer

Local Pickup or Store Pickup

In-Store Pickup:Creed Cart offers an in-store pickup option for customers in nearby areas to pick up their orders without additional shipping charges.
